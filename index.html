Whatcom County DSA is an official chapter of the Democratic Socialists of America. We believe that working people should run both the economy and society democratically — to meet human needs, not to make profits for a few.

Join the DSA

About Us

We believe that there is more to politics than just the ballot box. We are anti-capitalists who believe a better world is not only possible, but necessary. We work in solidarity with other community groups to advance our common goals.
